Father's Day

Third Sunday of June, every year

Father, the true hero of children life who can give his all efforts on the betterment them. He is the person the most efficient and a child can blindly trust a lot. We celebrate this day to make our students realize the importance of him.

One who leads in a right direction is our father. With respect to each and every father, father's day is celebrated. On this day our L.K.G and U.K.G students will greet their fathers whole heartedly.

They make greetings with their own little hands to welcome their fathers on the event.
