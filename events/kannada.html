Karnataka Rajyotsava

November 1st, every year

On 1st November we celebrate 'KANNADA RAJYOTSAVA' with a great pleasure.

There will be march in a dignified manner. Along with the band set , Kannada flag and a stablo of all time achievers of Kannada gave more beauty for the celebration . We dedicated our whole event to the late Puneet Rajkumar sir on his selfless social work .

On this event we honour the Karnataka Ratana, Dr. Puneeth Rajkumar with a cut out of 3 and half feet

All these activities are done under the guidance of all our faculties.
