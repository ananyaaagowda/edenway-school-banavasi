Grandparent's Day

First Sunday of September, every year

We also celebrate grandparent's day on first Monday of September(as Sunday will be holiday). Grandparents play a vital role in each and every child's life.

On behalf of school, the senior member of the family will be invited . All the grandparents will be participating in several games and cultural activities and will be awarded too. They will enjoy it a lot with their grandchildren.

The LKG and UKG students will be dressed as their grandparents.

All the grandparents will be heartly welcomed to the show

The winners will be awarded with prize.
