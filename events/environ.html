World Environmental Day

June 5th, every year

On 5th June,we celebrate World Environment day, every year. The various activities like Debate, dance and short play also will be organised. Children will be made consious about the safeguarding of our Environment.

We will make our students to prepare their own handmade crafts.

Paintings, charts will also be done by students about the Environment on this day.

Every student is made to draw a drawing using color pencil, crayon , pencil or paint. Prize will be given to the most beautiful drawing

All these activities are held and conducted under the guidance of all the faculties present.
