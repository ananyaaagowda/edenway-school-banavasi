The Independence Day

August 15th, every year

On 15th of August we will celebrate 'The Independence day' in enthusiastically in our school.

There will be many activities conducted on this day such as crafts.

There will be fashion show competition held on this day. Winners will be awarded with prize.

Early morning assembly and flag hoisting. Sweets will be distributed to students.

We made a length of 30meters India Flag . And our children of Edenway along with the teachers will carry it through out the main street with a patriotic march.

The people of Banavasi and all the dignitaries express their heartfelt appreciation . It was a feast to all our eyes.
