Teacher's Day

September 5th, every year

Behind every successful person there is a great hand of dedicative teacher. A student succeeds by following the right path which has been created by his teacher. The teacher plays a vital role in every individual's life. To showcase this, on 5th September Edenway celebrates the teacher's day with a meaningful manner.

That day our students will conduct several cultural programs and exciting game for their beloved teachers.
