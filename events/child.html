Children's Day

November 14th, every year

On this exciting day our little flowers of Edenway will come in colour dress. Several cultural events like singing, dancing, speech will also be performed by the teachers and student will really enjoy their day.

We will arrange meal to them. In the afternoon, we will host a number of games and whole day will be spent with complete joy and excitements.

Teachers will dress up as cartoon to make our students happy.
