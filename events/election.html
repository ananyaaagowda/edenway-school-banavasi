Election Day

When the school reopens the school cabinet election is conducted as per the parliament election of India. The leaders of the school would be elected which gives the political idea of our country.

Date of election, filing the nomination, withdrawing date declaration, candidates symbol, canvasing, poll and results declaration everything will be done on the same day.

Every teacher in the school can participate in voting session

Every students are made to stand in line and are given permission to vote one by one to their desired candidate
