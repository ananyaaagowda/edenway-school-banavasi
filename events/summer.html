Summer Camp

April or May, every year

Under the guidance of Administrator, guider, well wisher Mrs.Sanjida Shumaila maam we will organize Summer Camp in the summer holidays. It is a great success.

The camp is special, other than the daily curricular activities there will be different skills which will be taught to students like spoken English , mind games which develop their thinking capacities ,yoga, craft, drawing, art and introduction to tradition and several other activities will be conducted.

Such kind of camp enhances the skills and will give them sweet lifetime memories which will be cherished forever. Under the guidance of Shumaila maam, our teachers are successful in making the summer camp memorable.

This helps the students in their intellectual development
