The Past

EDENWAY High School was established in 2008 by Dr.Abdul Rauf Agro Research organization in Banavasi. During establishment it consisted only 2 grades with 35+ students in each class. We are grateful for all the parents who believed in us and joined their students to our school. At the begining, the school had only 1 hall, which was divided into 2 different rooms and the classes were conducted.



Now, this small school is developed so much that it has all classes from L.K.G to 10th Standard accommodating overall 450+ Students and nearly 20 teachers. Initially, it had only 1 playground where both prayer and sports was conducted. Formerly, it has well conditioned 2 playgrounds. The school is co-educational and an English medium school.

Therefore, the school has all the facilities required for a student to study and is convinient to approach in all the weather conditions by road. The academic session starts in April. The school is located in Rural area which is a perfect environment for a student to study in all the circumstances.
